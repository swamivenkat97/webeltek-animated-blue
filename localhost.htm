Frequently Asked Questions

How do you ensure the right technical fit?

We combine role scoping with multi-stage technical screenings, practical assessments, and stakeholder interviews to validate both skill and culture fit.

Can you support temporary, contract-to-hire, and full-time roles?

Yes. We deliver flexible engagement models—contract, C2H, and permanent hiring—backed by on-time payroll and compliant documentation.

How fast can you staff a role?

For common roles, shortlists are typically shared within days. Niche roles may take longer, but we keep you updated with clear milestones.

Do you operate internationally?

We support clients across India and internationally via digital delivery, remote teams, and on-site deployments where needed.

How does your payroll service integrate with our systems?

We’re API-first. We integrate with leading HRIS/ATS/timekeeping tools and can export custom reports in CSV/XLS/PDF.
